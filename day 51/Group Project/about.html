About Our Camp

Learn • Train • Grow • Make Friends

🏕 Our Mission

Camp Bakuriani combines sports, programming, and adventure in a safe environment where children learn new skills, build confidence, and create unforgettable memories.

💻 Programming & AI

Every day campers participate in programming and Artificial Intelligence lessons, learning modern technology while having fun.

🏋 Sports Training

Professional coaches lead CrossFit, self-defense, football, volleyball, wrestling basics, grappling, and striking sessions.

🍽 Full Package

The camp includes transportation, delicious meals, accommodation, 24/7 supervision, and daily activities.

🎯 Activities

Volleyball, football, tug-of-war, quizzes, karaoke, dancing, board games, and many exciting team challenges.

🛡 Safety

Our staff provides around-the-clock care and supervision to ensure every camper enjoys a safe and memorable experience.
