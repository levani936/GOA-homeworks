Lamborghini huracane

The Lamborghini Huracán (Spanish for "hurricane"; [uɾakan]) is a sports car built by Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini from 2014 to 2024.[6][7]
The Huracán was revealed online in December 2013,[8] making its worldwide debut at the 2014 Geneva Auto Show[9]
and was released to the market in the second quarter of 2014, replacing the Gallardo.
