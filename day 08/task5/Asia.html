Asia

Asia is the largest continent in the world by both land area and population.
It covers an area of more than 44 million square kilometres, about 30% of Earth's total land area and 8% of Earth's total surface area. The continent, which has long been home to the majority of the human population,[12] was the site of many of the first civilisations.
Its 4.7 billion people constitute roughly 60% of the world's population.
